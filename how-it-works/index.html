Speed cubing

How it works

This speedcubing timer works in a really easy way. In the Home you need to hold space for few seconds. Once you have a green background you are free to start, as soon as the space key is not pressed, the timer will start. Now solve the rubki's cube as fast as you can. Once you've finished the cube hit the spacebar and the timer will stop and you'll get the result on screen.

Where i can find my attempts?

All the attempts you did are available in the left side on the screen. You'll find a table with the result, and the attempt number. You can sort the attempt by the time taken or the attempt number.
You can even delete certain attempts, or view more info by clicking the relative icons.

What are the meaning of those letters?

If you are new to speedcubing, you probably wondering what letters like: "L, R, U.." and more means. Those are abbreviations of the moves. For instance, the L means a turn clockwise on the left of 90 degrees. Same with U, and R. The list of all the definitions is on this page
