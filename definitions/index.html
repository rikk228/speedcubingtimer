Speed cubing

Definitions

On this page you'll find all the definitions/notations used by all cubers around the world as well as the WCA. All the rotations are intended to be a 90° rotation.
This is the list for a regular NxNxN cube. If you have a different cube checkout this page

Face Moves

(outer slice)

  1. F - Front face
  2. B - Back Face
  3. R - Right Face
  4. L - Left Face
  5. U - Upper face
  6. D - Bottom (Down) face

Count clockwise rotations are identified with a " ' " after the rotation: " U', B', R', L', F', D' "

A double rotation is identified with a " 2 " before the rotation: " 2U, 2B, 2R, 2L, 2F, 2D "

Outer block moves

(outer slice with adjacent inner slices). For each of the moves defined below, n is the total number of slices to move, which must be in the range 1 < n < N (where N is the number of layers in the puzzle). n may also be omitted, for an implicit value of n=2 slices

  1. nFw
  2. nBw
  3. nRw
  4. nLw
  5. nUw
  6. nDw

Count clockwise rotations are identified with a " ' " after the rotation: " nUw', nBw', nRw', nLw', nFw', nDw' "

A double rotation is identified with a " 2 " after the rotation: " nUw2, nBw2, nRw2, nLw2, nFw2, nDw2 "

Rotations

(entire puzzle)

  1. X - same direction of R or L' is the same
  2. Y - same direction of U or D' is the same
  3. Z - same direction of F or B' is the same

Count clockwise rotations are identified with a " ' " after the rotation. "X - R' or L", "Y - U' or D", "Z - F' or B"

A double rotation is identified with a " 2 " after the rotation: "x2, y2, z2"
